Delete Your Account

Last updated: May 9, 2026

You have the right to delete your SwiftSolve account and all associated personal data at any time. This page explains how to do so and what data will be removed.

⚠ Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed.

Option 1: Delete from the App (Recommended)

The fastest way to delete your account is directly from within the SwiftSolve app:

  1. Open the SwiftSolve app on your device.
  2. Tap your profile icon or go to Settings.
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account".
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Option 2: Request Deletion by Email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us. Please include:

  • The email address associated with your SwiftSolve account
  • Your in-game nickname (if known)
  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"

Send your deletion request to:

info@swiftsolvegame.com

Send Deletion Request Email

We will process your request within 30 days and send a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

What Data Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your email address and display name (nickname)
  • Profile avatar and settings
  • All game scores, statistics, and history
  • Friend connections and friend requests
  • Multiplayer game records
  • World Arena tournament history
  • In-app purchase and Pro membership status (subscription itself must be cancelled separately via App Store / Google Play)

What Data May Be Retained

Some anonymized, non-personal data may be retained for legal or operational purposes (e.g., fraud prevention, aggregate analytics). This data cannot be used to identify you.

Cancel Your Subscription

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel an active Pro membership subscription. Please cancel your subscription separately:

  • iOS: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → SwiftSolve Pro → Cancel
  • Android: Google Play Store → Subscriptions → SwiftSolve Pro → Cancel

Questions?

If you have any questions about account deletion or your data, contact us at info@swiftsolvegame.com.
